The Problem

Dispatchers are deciding blind.

Route decisions get made on instinct, driver WhatsApp messages, and basic maps. There is no intelligence layer that connects weather, traffic, and road conditions before the truck leaves.

01
Unpredictable weather delays
Heavy rainfall on Pantura, flash floods in Sumatra — no one sees it coming at the route level before the truck is already stuck.
Rp 2–5 juta per incident
02
Poor dispatch timing decisions
Fleets dispatch at the worst possible window — when congestion and weather risk peak together — simply because no one knows the better alternative.
+3–6 hrs wasted per trip
03
No route-level visibility
Alternate routes that are faster today go unused. Road quality issues stay invisible. Seasonal congestion spikes come as a surprise every year.
8–15% fuel wasted

If 2 of these 3 situations feel familiar, your dispatch process needs better intelligence.
