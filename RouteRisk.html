The Problem

Dispatchers are deciding blind.

Route decisions get made on instinct, driver messages, and basic maps. No intelligence layer connects weather, traffic, and road conditions before the truck leaves.

01
Unpredictable weather delays
Heavy rainfall on Pantura, flash floods in Sumatra — no one sees it coming at route level before the truck is already stuck.
Rp 2–5 juta per incident
02
Poor dispatch timing decisions
Fleets dispatch at the worst possible window — when congestion and weather risk peak together — because no one knows the better alternative.
+3–6 hrs wasted per trip
03
No route-level visibility
Alternate routes go unused. Road quality issues stay invisible. Seasonal congestion spikes come as a surprise every year.
8–15% fuel wasted
The Solution

One score. One decision.
Fewer delays.

RouteRisk aggregates fragmented public data into one processed intelligence layer — built specifically for Indonesia's logistics corridors.
